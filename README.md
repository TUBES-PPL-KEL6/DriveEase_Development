## About Laravel

Laravel is a web application framework with expressive, elegant syntax. We believe development must be an enjoyable and creative experience to be truly fulfilling. Laravel takes the pain out of development by easing common tasks used in many web projects, such as:

- [Simple, fast routing engine](https://laravel.com/docs/routing).
- [Powerful dependency injection container](https://laravel.com/docs/container).
- Multiple back-ends for [session](https://laravel.com/docs/session) and [cache](https://laravel.com/docs/cache) storage.
- Expressive, intuitive [database ORM](https://laravel.com/docs/eloquent).
- Database agnostic [schema migrations](https://laravel.com/docs/migrations).
- [Robust background job processing](https://laravel.com/docs/queues).
- [Real-time event broadcasting](https://laravel.com/docs/broadcasting).

Laravel is accessible, powerful, and provides tools required for large, robust applications.

# DriveEase

## Project Overview
DriveEase is a web-based vehicle rental platform designed to address mobility needs while supporting sustainable development goals. The platform connects vehicle owners/rental companies with customers who need temporary transportation solutions, focusing specifically on four-wheeled vehicles (cars).

## Core Purpose
The system aims to modernize the traditional vehicle rental process by digitizing operations that were previously manual, including vehicle search, booking, payment processing, document verification, and customer reviews.

## Key Objectives
- Increase accessibility and transparency in vehicle rental services
- Support digital economic growth by creating business opportunities for rental providers
- Optimize vehicle utilization efficiency
- Build a more inclusive transportation ecosystem

## Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Support
- **SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth):** Creating new job opportunities in vehicle rental
- **SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure):** Providing transportation infrastructure for mobility needs
- **SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities):** Reducing private vehicle ownership dependency
- **SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production):** Promoting resource-efficient consumption patterns

## Core Features Breakdown
### 1. User Authentication & Profiles
- **User Registration:** Account creation with personal data and document verification
- **User Login:** Email/password authentication with Google integration option
- **Profile Management:** Users can update personal information and verification documents
- **Multi-role System:** Different interfaces for customers, rental companies, and administrators

### 2. Vehicle Search & Booking
- **Advanced Search:** Filter vehicles by location, price, type, and availability
- **Detailed Vehicle Information:** Specifications, pricing, availability calendars
- **Booking System:** Reservation process with date selection and pricing calculation
- **Driver Services:** Option to book with or without a driver

### 3. Payment Processing
- **Multiple Payment Methods:** E-wallet, bank transfers, and QRIS support
- **Payment Status Tracking:** Real-time payment verification and confirmation
- **Automated Notifications:** Payment confirmations and receipts

### 4. Document Verification & Security
- **ID Verification:** KTP (ID card) and SIM (driver's license) verification process
- **Document Management:** Secure storage and processing of identification documents
- **Verification Status:** Real-time status updates on document verification

### 5. Review & Rating System
- **User Reviews:** Customers can review vehicles and service quality
- **Company Reviews:** Rental companies can review customer behavior
- **Rating Management:** Administrators can monitor and moderate reviews

### 6. Dashboards
- **Customer Dashboard:** Booking history, recommended vehicles, payment status
- **Rental Company Dashboard:** Statistics on bookings, income, and vehicle performance
- **Admin Dashboard:** User management, transaction monitoring, suspicious activity alerts

### 7. Driver Management
- **Driver Profiles:** Information and availability management for drivers
- **Driver Scheduling:** Management of driver assignments and availability
- **Driver Reviews:** Rating and feedback for driver services

### 8. Booking History & Tracking
- **Comprehensive History:** Complete record of past bookings for all users
- **Transaction Records:** Detailed information on all financial transactions
- **Data Analytics:** Trend analysis and historical data for business intelligence

## User Roles & Access
### 1. Customers
- Search and book vehicles
- Make payments
- Submit reviews and ratings
- View booking history
- Update personal profiles
- Choose optional driver services

### 2. Rental Companies
- Manage vehicle inventory
- Process bookings and returns
- View income reports and statistics
- Manage driver availability
- Review customer behavior

### 3. Administrators
- Validate user data and documents
- Monitor platform transactions
- Handle user disputes
- Generate system reports
- Manage overall platform security
